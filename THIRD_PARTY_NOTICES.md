# Third-party notices

Shyne Creator depends on Fabric Loader/Fabric API or NeoForge, Minecraft mappings/tooling, Gson, LuaJ, and the optional Simple Voice Chat API. Their licenses and distribution terms remain with their respective projects.

No files from local research or reference directories are intentionally distributed with Shyne Creator. Those directories must remain outside Git and release artifacts.

Before a public release, generate a dependency license report and complete `ASSET_PROVENANCE.md` for every bundled binary asset.
